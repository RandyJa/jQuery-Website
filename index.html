Commonly Asked Questions

What language is spoken in Italy?

Italian is the official language of Italy although there are a wide variety of languages and dialects spoken!

Do I need a visa to travel to Italy?

U.S. citizens may enter Italy for up to 90 days for tourist or business purposes without a visa. All non-residents are required to complete a declaration of presence.

When is the best time to travel to Italy?

  • May
  • June
  • September
  • October

Pleasant weather, vibrant scenery, and lower costs are just a few reasons why these are the most popular months to visit.

What currency is used in Italy?

Euros are the currency used in Italy as it is a robust international currency.
